Automating Product Photo Workflow

Ecommerce store BusyBabeShoppe - after intiial consultation and overview of BusyBabe workflow for posting new inventory to their etsy and shopify site - I noticed the store owner Rose, was manually watermarking photos in photoshop - which was extremely time costly and downright painful.

I proposed an automated workflow that would allow importing a folder of un-watermarked item photos into photoshop and executing stored photoshop actions script that would perform new layer generation, waterark import and placement at set X,Y coordinates, then export the file as JPG to a automatically created ooutput folder made inside of the input folder.-backdrop-hue-rotate-15

this made an exponential impact in roses time and quality of life by reducing the tedious processe of manually importing images, manually watermark import and placement, manual export as JPG and storage. A process that would take hours over the course of several days - reduced to a few minutes and few clicks
